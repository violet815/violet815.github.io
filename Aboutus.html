About Us

Driven by purpose, powered by ICT — we empower communities through digital knowledge and creative problem-solving.

Mission

To educate and inform about ICT by making digital skills accessible and practical for everyone in Kenya and beyond.

Vision

To empower individuals and institutions with knowledge and ICT solutions that enable them to thrive in the digital era.

History

Since 2020, Violet Wachira has led a mission to bridge the digital gap. What started as a personal vision grew into a national consultancy serving students, businesses, and institutions with ICT training and support.
